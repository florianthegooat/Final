About Fokus Kosova

Your trusted partner in premium upholstery solutions since 2016

Our Mission

At Fokus Kosova, we are committed to providing the highest quality upholstery materials and components to furniture manufacturers, designers, and craftsmen across Kosovo and beyond.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern innovation, ensuring that every piece of furniture created with our materials meets the highest standards of quality, comfort, and durability.
